NRI Support

Overview

Managing finances in India while living abroad can be complex for Non-Resident Indians due to regulatory requirements, documentation procedures, and frequent changes in financial policies. Our NRI Financial Services in India are designed to provide complete, reliable, and compliant financial solutions under one roof. We assist NRIs with investments, banking, loans, and insurance services, ensuring transparency, convenience, and peace of mind.

We provide end-to-end support for all NRI banking needs in compliance with RBI regulations. Our services include:

  • Opening and managing NRE, NRO, and FCNR accounts
  • Conversion of resident accounts to NRI status
  • KYC updates and documentation assistance
  • Fixed deposits and recurring deposits
  • Fund transfers and remittance support
  • Digital banking setup and nominee registration
  • Guidance on repatriation of funds as per applicable rules

    • We coordinate directly with banks to ensure smooth transactions and timely resolution of issues.

Our investment services help NRIs build and manage wealth in India through compliant and well-structured investment options. We offer guidance based on individual financial goals and risk profiles, including:

  • Mutual fund investments (where permitted)
  • Fixed deposits and bonds
  • Government-approved investment schemes
  • Real estate investment coordination
  • Portfolio monitoring and review
  • Compliance with FEMA and taxation rules

    • Our objective is to help NRIs make informed investment decisions while ensuring long-term financial growth and security.

We assist NRIs in accessing various loan products available in India with complete documentation and professional support. Our loan services include:

Why NRIs Choose Us:
  • One-Stop Solution – for property purchase or construction
  • Loan against property
  • Education loans for children studying in India or abroad
  • Advisory on eligibility, interest rates, and repayment structures

We coordinate with banks and financial institutions to ensure transparent processing and timely approvals.

Insurance is a crucial part of financial planning, especially for NRIs with family and assets in India. We offer consultation and support for:

  • Life insurance and term plans
  • Health insurance for self and family members
  • Travel insurance
  • Property and asset insurance
  • Policy renewals and premium payment assistance
  • Claims support and coordination
