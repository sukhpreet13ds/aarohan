Overview

We offer comprehensive Import and Export Services exclusively designed for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) who wish to trade, ship, or move goods to and from India with confidence and ease. Navigating international trade regulations, customs procedures, and logistics can be complex—our experienced team ensures smooth, compliant, and timely execution of every shipment.

Your Trusted Import & Export Partner in India

Our import services help NRIs bring personal or commercial goods into India safely and legally, while ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and customs regulations.

Our Import Services Include:
  • Import documentation preparation
  • Customs clearance and duty assessment
  • Import-export code (IEC) assistance
  • Shipment tracking and coordination
  • PAN card, Aadhaar, and other ID documentation support
  • Warehousing and storage support
  • Door-to-door delivery services
Benefits of Our Import Services:
  1. Regulatory Compliance – Adherence to Indian customs and DGFT guidelines
  2. Cost Transparency – Clear duty structure and charges
  3. Time Efficiency – Reduced delays through expert handling
  4. End-to-End Support – From origin to final delivery

We assist NRIs and overseas businesses in exporting Indian goods to global destinations. Our export solutions are customized based on product type, destination country, and regulatory requirements.

Our Export Services Include:
  • Export documentation and invoicing
  • Packing list and certificate preparation
  • Customs clearance and cargo handling
  • Freight forwarding (air, sea, and road)
  • Export incentives and compliance support
  • Real-time shipment monitoring
Products We Commonly Handle:
  1. Handicrafts and textiles
  2. Spices and agricultural products
  3. Industrial goods and machinery
  4. Consumer goods and merchandise

We understand the unique challenges NRIs face when managing trade from abroad. Our services are designed to bridge the distance and simplify operations.

Why NRIs Trust Our Import & Export Services:
  1. Single Point of Contact – Dedicated trade manager for every client
  2. Remote Coordination – Manage shipments without visiting India
  3. Trusted Network – Reliable customs agents and logistics partners
  4. Risk Management – Proper documentation to avoid penalties or delays

International trade requires strict adherence to laws, quality standards, and timelines. We ensure your business meets all legal and procedural requirements.

Our Commitment:
  1. Accurate and timely documentation
  2. Compliance with international trade laws
  3. Ethical business practices
  4. Regular updates and transparent communication

Whether you are importing personal belongings, exporting Indian products, or setting up an international trading operation, our NRI Import & Export Services provide dependable solutions under one roof. We act as your local representative in India, handling complexities while you focus on growth and global opportunities.
